About CN-TECH Academy Uganda

CN-TECH ACADEMY UGANDA was founded in May 2023 by visionary legend Muwanguzi Jonathan.
He developed the idea after a C programming exam, realizing that Metropolitan International University had great computer scientists.
He later implemented the idea with his class, who agreed it was a great initiative, similar to those in top programming camps and schools.
