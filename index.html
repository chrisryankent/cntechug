CN-TECH ACADEMY UGANDA

The tech academy Uganda was formed on 29th of September 2023. It was formed with the aim of solving all computer-related problems. We deal in all different types of software updates and upgrades, graphics designing, website development, application development, computer care and maintenance, system analysis, database management among others.

OUR VISION is to offer a wide range of solutions to the world's problems using technology ... and OUR MISSION is to reduce the bridging gap between computer illiterate and literate all over the world, by teaching and equipping both IT native and IT immigrants with skills of ICT.

Services Offered
Web Designing

We design and develop websites in a specified time possible and also teach students how to design their own websites from first principles using languages such as HTML, CSS, JAVA, PHP, and many more. On top of these, we teach them digital marketing skills.

Mobile Application Development

We develop applications for Android, Windows, Mac, Linux, among others. We also teach students to create these applications using languages such as Kotlin, C#, JavaScript, Python, among others.

Database Management

We monitor and develop database systems for schools, hospitals, companies, among others.

Systems Design

We design database systems for schools, hospitals, companies, among others.
